# Conclusion

By projecting AD GWAS signals onto whole-body spatial transcriptomics across 15 organs and 10 age points, we identified two mechanistically distinct peripheral pathways of AD genetic risk: a myeloid/immune pathway active in tissue-resident macrophages across all organs, and an APP processing pathway selectively active in colonic epithelial cells. The gut epithelial enrichment was AD-specific and absent in PD, providing spatial genetic evidence that host intestinal cells — not only the microbiome — contribute to the gut-brain axis in AD.
